Artist Statement

My work explores the layered histories and spiritual legacies embedded in South African soil. Drawing from personal memory, cultural identity, and collective ancestry, I reimagine symbols, figures, and artifacts in order to challenge colonial narratives and honor living traditions. Through mixed media, I weave together the tactile and the ephemeral – pigment, charcoal, pastel – with stories that evoke both resistance and resilience. Each artwork is a bridge between time periods, generations, and the ongoing dialogue between past and present.
